About the Game

Tom Clancy’s Rainbow Six Siege is a tactical first-person shooter developed by Ubisoft. The game rewards planning, communication, and smart gadget use.

Matches are usually 5v5, with attackers trying to breach and complete an objective while defenders hold key positions.
